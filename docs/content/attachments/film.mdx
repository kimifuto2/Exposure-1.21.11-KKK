## Renaming
You can quickly rename the film by ***using*** the item.\
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;• Renaming this way does not cost experience and can be of a great help to identify/organize rolls.\
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;• Renamed rolls keep their new name when developed.

## Developing
<Asset location="exposure:developed_black_and_white_film" width={50}/>**Developed Black and White Film**\
<Asset location="exposure:developed_color_film" width={50}/>**Developed Color Film**

When you are finished with the roll, you need to develop it to make it suitable for printing in [Lightroom](../lightroom).

<Asset location="exposure:black_and_white_film" width={50}/> **Black and White Films** are developed by crafting the roll with just a single **Water Bottle**.\
<Asset location="exposure:color_film" width={50}/> **Color Films** are developed by crafting the roll with **Awkward Potion**, **Thick Potion** and **Mundane Potion**.
